We offer a range of cylinders with unique and user-friendly features while establishing an unrivalled safety record including the composite lightweight LPG cylinder with the following features:

Lightweight
The innovative composite material used in the hexagon ragasco LPG Cylinders offer an ideal weight which is up to 2 times lighter than conventional steel gas cylinders.

User friendly
The transparent body makes it easy to see how much gas you have left while the ergonomic handle and light weight of the bottles which allows easier transport and helps to prevent injuries for users and handlers.

Safe
Unparalleled safety with more than 15 million users world wide the cylinders of hexagon ragasco do not even explode under extreme pressure and fire but, slowly release the gas in a controlled manner.

Durable & Rust free
The Extremely durable and rust free material offers long usage helps reduce maintenance costs and prevents dirty rust stains for users and companies.
